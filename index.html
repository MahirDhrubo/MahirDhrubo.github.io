Research

Revolutionising Traditional Mouza Map Digitization Method in Bangladesh: A Paradigm Shift with Machine Learning Having Human in The Loop

June 2022 - Current

Undergraduate Thesis
Mouza maps (cadastral maps) of Bangladesh are hand-drawn and digitized version of maps are highly useful for applications such as land management, urban planning, and geographical analysis. Current manual digitization methods are time-consuming and labor-intensive. Our study proposes a semi-automated approach to streamline the digitization process, saving both time and human resources. Our approach demonstrated time savings of up to 48%. We have developed a deep learning network for pre-processing, pipeline for vectorization and smoothing algorithm for post process.

Field: Computer Vision, Machine learning, Image Processing
